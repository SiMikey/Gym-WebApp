LEG Exercises

Exercise 1: Squats

Squats are a fundamental lower body exercise that strengthens hips, glutes, quads, and hamstrings.

  1. Stand with feet wider than hip-width apart, toes facing front.
  2. Drive hips back, knees bend slightly outward.
  3. Keep heels down, chest up, and lower until thighs are parallel.
  4. Press through heels and return to standing.

Exercise 2: Side-Lying Left Leg Lift

This bodyweight move targets your hips, glutes, hamstrings, and lower back.

  1. Lie on your right side with legs stacked and extended.
  2. Support your head with your right arm, left hand on hip.
  3. Exhale and raise your left leg until you feel tension.

Exercise 3: Walking Lunges

This move challenges balance and works quads, hamstrings, and glutes.

  1. Start standing, feet together, holding dumbbells if needed.
  2. Step forward with right leg into a lunge.
  3. Push up and bring legs together again.
  4. Repeat on the left leg and continue walking forward.

Exercise 4: Glute Bridge

A great exercise to activate glutes and strengthen your posterior chain using only body weight.

  1. Lie on your back with knees bent and feet flat.
  2. Press into your heels and lift hips toward ceiling.
  3. Pause at the top, then lower with control.

Exercise 5: Goblet Squat

An easier variation of barbell squats that still targets quads and glutes effectively.

  1. Hold a dumbbell vertically close to your chest.
  2. Lower into a squat, keeping the dumbbell in contact with your body.
  3. Push through heels and return to standing.

Exercise 6: Bulgarian Split Squat

This unilateral move builds strength and balance by isolating one leg at a time.

  1. Place one foot on a bench behind you and hold weight at your chest.
  2. Lower into a lunge, keeping shoulders above hips.
  3. Push through the front heel to return to standing.
  4. Repeat on both sides.
