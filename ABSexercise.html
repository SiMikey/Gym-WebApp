ABS EXERCISES

Exercise 1: Hardstyle Plank

Do it: Lying face down on the ground, prop yourself up into a forearm plank position...

  1. Get into an elbow plank position.
  2. Fists clenched, feet together.
  3. Brace abs and squeeze glutes.
  4. Hold 10–30 seconds, breathe steadily.

Exercise 2: Dead Bug

Do it: Lie face up, arms above shoulders, alternate arms/legs lowering. Do 14 alternating reps.

  1. Lie flat, arms/legs up.
  2. Lower opposite arm and leg.
  3. Return, switch sides.
  4. Repeat until set is complete.

Exercise 3: Hollow Extension-to-Cannonball

Do it: Hug knees to chest, extend arms and legs into hollow, then return. Hold 5 secs, 5 reps.

  1. Lie flat, legs extended.
  2. Engage core and raise legs.
  3. Extend arms behind head.
  4. Hold position, then return.

Exercise 4: Dumbbell Side Bend

Do it: Hold a dumbbell on one side, bend to the same side, return to start. 12–20 reps per set.

  1. Stand tall, dumbbell in right hand.
  2. Bend to the right, pause, return.
  3. Repeat and switch sides.

Exercise 5: Barbell Back Squat

Do it: Hold barbell on back, squat deeply, press through heels. 12 reps per set.

  1. Barbell across upper back.
  2. Squat down to 90° angle.
  3. Push back up to stand.

Exercise 6: Bird Dog

Do it: From tabletop, extend opposite arm and leg, touch elbow to knee, repeat both sides.

  1. Extend right arm + left leg.
  2. Touch elbow to knee below body.
  3. Repeat with opposite side.
  4. Complete 5 reps each side.
