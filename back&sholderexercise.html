BACK & SHOULDER EXERCISES

Pull Up

  1. Grip the bar with palms facing away, slightly wider than shoulder-width.
  2. Keep your chest up and eyes on the bar.
  3. Pull yourself up until chin clears the bar.
  4. Lower with control until arms are fully extended.

Barbell Rows

  1. Grip the bar with an overhand grip.
  2. Bend forward and let the bar hang from straight arms.
  3. Pull the bar towards your abs or chest.
  4. Lower the bar slowly back to the start.

Overhead Press

  1. Set the barbell at chest height on a rack.
  2. Grip it slightly wider than shoulder-width.
  3. Unrack the bar and step back.
  4. Press overhead to full extension.
  5. Lower to shoulder height and repeat.

Dumbbell Lateral Raise

  1. Hold dumbbells at sides with almost straight arms.
  2. Lift arms out to shoulder height.
  3. Lower with control and repeat.

Back Extensions

  1. Adjust machine height to support thighs.
  2. Place feet shoulder-width and rest upper body forward.
  3. Cross arms or place hands behind head.
  4. Engage lower back to lift torso to a straight line.
  5. Hold briefly, then lower with control.
