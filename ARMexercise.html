ARM EXERCISES

Exercise 1: Barbell Bicep Curl

This exercise uses the barbell to build muscle mass in both arms simultaneously and is excellent for gaining strength.

  1. Stand shoulder-width apart holding a barbell with underhand grip.
  2. Curl the bar to your chest, keep elbows close and core tight.
  3. Hold the top contraction briefly and feel your biceps.

Exercise 2: Dumbbell Preacher Curl

This is a great bicep isolation move, allowing one-arm focus to balance strength on both sides.

  1. Place upper arm on preacher/incline bench holding dumbbell.
  2. Lower slowly to full stretch.
  3. Curl back to shoulder height, pinky higher than thumb.
  4. Squeeze at top, then repeat. Switch arms.

Exercise 3: Skull Crusher

Also known as the lying triceps extension. It isolates the triceps and improves upper arm mass and tone.

  1. Lower dumbbell toward forehead or behind head with bent elbows.
  2. Keep upper arms perpendicular to body throughout.
  3. Push back up without locking elbows.
  4. Repeat carefully with control.

Exercise 4: Bent-Over Tricep Extension

  1. Bend over holding dumbbells with elbows at sides.
  2. Extend arms fully behind you, squeezing triceps.
  3. Return to start and repeat.

Exercise 5: Tricep Dip

Bodyweight tricep move that can be done using a chair or bench to build strong arms and shoulders.

  1. Lift and slide hips off the edge of chair.
  2. Lower body to 90-degree elbow angle.
  3. Push up slowly and repeat.

Exercise 6: Front to Lateral Raises

This combo targets the shoulder muscles (deltoids), enhancing arm size and definition without heavy strain.

  1. Hold dumbbells by your sides.
  2. Raise laterally to shoulder height, then lower.
  3. Raise directly in front of you, then lower.
  4. Repeat the full sequence.
